My Skills and Hobbies.

I built my first PC when I was 14, ever since I've had a fascination with constructing, modifying and upgrading computers. Networking

During the first semester at of my Masters at Griffith University I was introduced to the fundamentals of networking, and was quickly engaged with understanding how enterprises connect their users, exchange data and run business processes. I've since begun studying towards my CompTIA Network+ Certification, with the goal of developing a deeper grasp of physical and virtual networks, the protocols that underpin them and the modern transition towards the Cloud.

Data Analysis

After being first being exposed to beautiful BI dashboards during my Undergraduate majoring in Marketing, I wanted to learn for myself the techniques of analysing data. First I started with understanding relational database design; then mastered complex SQL queries to gather data; R and Python tools like Pandas to manipulate and analyse complex datasets; while dabbling into Machine Learning to create predictions; until I finally made my way to building dashboards with software like Tableau and PowerBI. I find the Data Analytics space fascinating from top to bottom.
